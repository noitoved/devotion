A Selection of Curatorial Works

Conjuring - Ben Russell Performance and Films
Kunsthal Charlottenborg, October - November 2018


CONJURING is a live AV performance in which analogue synthesis becomes a spell for conjuring, a tool for geomancy. A mountain emerges, an invisible summit appears. We are earth and we are mountain and we climb forever upwards along a chaos of slopes where synaesthesia is feeling, mapping is modeling, volume (sound) is volume (space), and waveform is mountain song.

States of Desolation Film Programme
Kunsthal Charlottenborg, April 2018

A selection of films about the desolated states of human nature with a backdrop of history, isolation, local myths and speculations. Films made by Ali Cherri, John Skoog, Maria von Hausswolff and Anne Gry Friis Kristensen.
